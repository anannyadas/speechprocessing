                    BILL PRESENTED TO THE PRESIDENT

  Karen L. Haas, Clerk of the House, reported that on November 2, 2017, 
she presented to the President of the United States, for his approval, 
the following bill:

       H.R. 1329. To increase, effective as of December 1, 2017, 
     the rates of compensation for veterans with service-connected 
     disabilities and the rates of dependency and indemnity 
     compensation for the survivors of certain disabled veterans, 
     and for other purposes.
