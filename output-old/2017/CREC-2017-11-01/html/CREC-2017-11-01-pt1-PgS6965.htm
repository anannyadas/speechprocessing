                            VOTE EXPLANATION

 Mr. MENENDEZ. Mr. President, I was unavailable for rollcall 
vote No. 257, on the nomination of Joan Larsen, of Michigan, to be U.S. 
circuit judge for the Sixth Circuit. Had I been present, I would have 
voted nay.
  Mr. President, I was unavailable for rollcall vote No. 258, on the 
motion to invoke cloture on Allison Eid, of Colorado, to be U.S. 
circuit judge for the Tenth Circuit. Had I been present, I would have 
voted nay.
