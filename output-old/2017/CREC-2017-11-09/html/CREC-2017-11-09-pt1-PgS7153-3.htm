                            VOTE EXPLANATION

 Mr. TESTER. Mr. President, I was necessarily absent due to a 
family funeral for the votes on confirmation of Executive Calendar No. 
407 and the motion to invoke cloture on Executive Calendar No. 159.
  On vote No. 268, had I been present, I would have voted nay on the 
confirmation of Executive Calendar No. 407.
  On vote No. 269, had I been present, I would have voted yea on the 
motion to invoke cloture on Executive Calendar No. 159.
