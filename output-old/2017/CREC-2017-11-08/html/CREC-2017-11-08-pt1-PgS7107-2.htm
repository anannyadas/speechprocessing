                            VOTE EXPLANATION

 Mr. TESTER. Mr. President, I was necessarily absent due to a 
family funeral for the votes on confirmation of Executive Calendar No. 
384 and the motion to invoke cloture on Executive Calendar No. 407.

confirmation of Executive Calendar No. 384.
  On vote No. 267, had I been present, I would have voted nay on the 
motion to invoke cloture on Executive Calendar No. 407.
